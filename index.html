Notes
- This mobile demo expects a normal image file (.jpg/.png), not a .npy array.
- The browser resizes the image to 224x224, normalizes pixels to 0-1, and converts it to CHW order to match the training pipeline.
- The model runs locally in the browser, so this is closer to real edge inference than opening Databricks on your phone.
